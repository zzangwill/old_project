Peach Berry Crumble
  • 1 cup quick oats
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup slivered almonds
  • 8 tbs (stick) unsalt butter, melt
  • 1 1/2 lb peaches (4-5)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup blubs, rasps
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • pinch of salt

Make topping: combine oats, flour, brown sugar, salt, cinnoman. Stir in butter until crumbly, toss in nuts.

Make filling: Bring pot to boil with bucket of ice ready. Cut 'X' into bottom of peach. Blanch ~5 minutes until wrinkly. Ice to cool and remove skin. Slice.

In bowl, toss peaches with sugar, flour, and salt. Fold in berries. Let stand 5 minutes.

Transfer to buttered baking dish. Sprinke with topping. Place on baking sheet, bake 350 for 50-55 minutes until bubbly and brown.
