Banana Bread
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 egg or 1/3 egg sub
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3/4 cup grated zucc
  • Choco chips

Mix oil, sugar, vanilla, and eggs.

Add banana.

Mix flour, baking soda, and salt seperately.

Add dry ingredients to wet, plus zucchini and chocolate chips.

Bake at 350 degrees for around 45 minutes.
