Thai Chicken Strips
  • Sliced chicken breasts
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Peanut butter
  • Fish sauce
  • Thai chili paste
  • Sesame oil
  • Olive oil
  • Chopped pecans/peanuts/bread crumbs

Marinate chicken strips in fridge.

Remove, roll around in coating/crumb mixture.

Preheat baking sheet with foil at 425.

Cook 10 minutes on each side.

Serve with veggie, fried rice, and sweet chili sauce.
