Chocolate Cake
  • 1 Duncan Hines Chocolate Cake Mix
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 instant chocolate chip pudding
  • 1/2 cup chocolate syrup
  • 9oz chocoalte chips

Mix duncan hines mix, sour cream, oil, warm water, eggs, chocolate pudding, and chocolate syrup together.

Gently add chocolate chips.

Cook at 325 degree Farenheit for 50-60 minutes.

Let cake cook until comes off the walks of bundt pan, then remove. Sprinkle with powdered sugar, and serve.
