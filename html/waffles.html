Waffles
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp to 1 tb sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 water + yeast
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda

Mix water and yeast, let rest 5 minutes

Mix flour, oil, oil, warm milk, salt, and sugar.

Add yeast mixture. Wisk to remove any clumps.

Let sit overnight covered at room temperature.

Next morning, add eggs and baking soda.

Cook with a waffle iron!
